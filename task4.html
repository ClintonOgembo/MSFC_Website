Rules and Regulations

General Conduct

  • Punctuality: All team members must arrive on time for practices, meetings, and games
  • Respect: Players must show respect to coaches, teammates, opponents, referees, and fans at all times
  • Sportsmanship: Demonstrate good sportsmanship on and off the field
    Unsportsmanlike conduct will not be tolerated

Training and Practices

Attendance: Regular attendance at all training sessions is mandatory. Absences must be communicated to the coach in advance
Effort and Attitude: Players are expected to give 100% effort during training and maintain a positive attitude
Equipment: Proper training gear and equipment must be worn during all practices

Game Day

Uniform: Players must wear the full team uniform, including any required protective gear
Warm-Up: Arrive early to participate in team warm-ups and pre-game strategies
Behavior: Follow the instructions of the coaching staff and exhibit appropriate behavior on the field

Health and Safety

Injuries: Report any injuries to the coach immediately. Do not play through serious injuries
Hydration and Nutrition: Maintain proper hydration and nutrition, especially on game days
Medical Clearances: Provide any necessary medical clearances or updates to the coaching staff

Team Meetings and Communication

Participation: Attend all team meetings and contribute to discussions
Communication: Keep open lines of communication with coaches and teammates. Notify the coach of any issues affecting your ability to participate

Social Media and Public Image

Social Media: Use social media responsibly. Do not post negative or harmful comments about the team, opponents, or officials
Public Conduct: Represent the team positively in all public settings

Disciplinary Actions

Consequences: Understand that violations of these rules can result in disciplinary actions, which may include suspension from practices or games, or even removal from the team
Appeals: Players have the right to appeal disciplinary actions through a formal process with the team management

Commitment
